Lurie Children’s

The Lurie Children’s Annual Report has transitioned from the traditional print format to an online only microsite. As the digital team lead, I have worked with the client stakeholders on strategy, and lead the design and development team.

The architecture of the site is similar each year, so the challenge is always to create a unique, dynamic online report, and find new ways to push the UX and enhance communication of the hospital’s mission.

Sterling Bay

Sterling Bay is a Chicago-based commercial real estate firm, perhaps best know for revitalizing the Fulton Market area of Chicago. The website was completed as part of an overall brand refresh.

In addition to directing the design team, I also handled the Drupal 8 development and theming. The website is a key touchpoint for the Sterling Bay brand and has been instrumental in winning new commercial real estate clients.

Design / Concept / UX / Wordpress

Skender

A recent Wordpress project, Skender Construction referenced the Sterling Bay website as the key reason they chose to work with Pivot Design. Initial discovery work with many client stakeholders, revealed that their current web site no longer reflected the company’s work or dynamic culture. The new site structure and design more truly reflect the amazing work they do, while also sharing their dynamic culture to attract high-quality job candidates. The project quickly evolved to a full rebrand based largely on the new website.

Design / Concept / UX / Wordpress

Priester Aviation

As a small private aviation company, one of the challenges was to create a digital presence to compete with their larger competitors, while keeping their family heritage a key differentiator. As the lead art director/designer, I was responsible for every aspect of this project. Beyond a website, this project included coordination and direction of a 2-day photo shoot. The site was designed before mobile-first was even a buzz word, it includes a feature for the client to list empty leg flights, and maintain detailed information about their fleet. An early Drupal 7 site which is still up and running!
